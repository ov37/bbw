Mission Statement

Why we're here

BeBetterWHS seeks to prevent sexual assault and harassment at school. By providing students and faculty with accessible resources and clear, candid education surrounding sexual assault and harassment, the Woodgrove High School administration can address its shortcomings and establish a new precedent for combatting sexual violence.
