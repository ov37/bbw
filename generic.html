Mission Statement

Why we are here

BeBetterWHS seeks to prevent sexual assault and harassment at the source (the source - school). By providing students and faculty with accessible resources and clear, candid education surrounding sexual assault and harassment, the Woodgrove High School administration can address its current shortcomings and establish a new precedent for combatting sexual violence.
